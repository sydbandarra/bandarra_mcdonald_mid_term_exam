Cold Weather

Why Cold Weather is Superior

Winter in Canada means snowy adventures, bundling up in cozy clothes, hot cocoa and cider, and plenty of fun and frosty activities to enjoy. Whether you're out on a sunny winter stroll, hitting the ski slopes, or snuggling up to a warm cup of tea in the comfort of your living room, the possibilities are endless in this Winter Wonderland.

Activities That You Can Enjoy in Cold Weather

  • Ice Skating

    • Ice Skating is a classic winter activity that you can enjoy solo or with that special someone, even try it with some friends! Whether you're a total pro or an absolute beginner, there is plenty of fun to be had when you go Ice Skating.

  • Ice Hockey
  • Build A Snowman
  • Snow Sports
    • Skiing
    • Snowboarding
    • Snow Mobiling
    • Snowball Fights
  • Sledding
  • Tubing
  • Enjoy A Winter Walk

    • Cold weather brings out the wooly winter coats, cuddly-soft sweaters, and those mittens that Grandma made you. There is no better way to enjoy the cold weather than to bundle up in cozy layers and enjoy a brisk Winter Walk with your loved ones.

What is The Best Part About Cold Weather?

After a long day of playing in the snow and exploring the frosty Winter Wonderland there is nothing more comforting than coming home and enjoying a hot drink on the couch under a warm blanket.
